What is Galaxy Defenders?

Welcome soldier!
If you are reading this document, it means that your physical or mental capabilities are excellent and our planet needs you!
You are joining a secret agency responsible for detecting and stopping alien activity on Earth... keep reading, if you dare, but keep in mind two simple rules:

  • They are already here!
  • They are not friendly!
You will be part of an elite team formed by up to 5 agents involved in critical, tactical missions. You will encounter hostile aliens, so be prepared to engage them with no mercy.

The Zeppelin is powered by a series of steam engines, which drive large propellers that provide the necessary lift and propulsion to keep the airship airborne. The engines also power a variety of weapons, including cannons, machine guns, and missiles, which are housed in armored turrets and gun ports along the length of the airship.
The weapons systems of the Zeppelin are among the most advanced in this world, with sophisticated targeting systems and advanced ballistics technology. The airship is also equipped with defensive measures, including smoke screens and chaff dispensers, which can be deployed to help evade enemy fire.

The cowboy's attire is a combination of rugged leather and metal armor, adorned with intricate gears and cogs.
His primary weapon is a plasma rifle, a powerful and destructive weapon that harnesses the power of plasma to obliterate his enemies. The rifle is a mix of brass, copper, and steel, and is adorned with intricate etchings and designs. He also uses a power fist, a mechanical glove that amplifies his strength and can crush metal with ease.

This sinister character is a ruthless and cruel gunslinger.
His weapon is a pistol of extraordinary power, a masterpiece of Steampunk engineering that harnesses the power of steam to fire bullets with incredible force. The pistol is a mix of brass, copper, and steel, adorned with intricate etchings and designs. The weapon's chamber is filled with a specially designed fuel that, when ignited, creates an explosion of steam that propels the bullet forward with devastating force.
