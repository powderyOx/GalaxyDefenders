This is your Agent!

For this demo the most important section is the GD4A1 Rifle.

The number next to the heart symbol is your HP.

The number next to the boot symbol is the number of hex-fields you can travel per round.

The EAGLE 1 is your secondary weapon. But we won't need it in this demo.

Step 1

You are the green point on the map.

For now your objective is to move towards the enemy and kill him.

Rotate (left mouse button), zoom (mouse wheel) and move (right mouse button) the map around to get an overview of the battlefield.

On the right side you can see your agent.

On the left you can see your enemy.

Lets Start!

Step 2

You (green point) moved towards your opponent (red point).

The enemy has now been revealed.

Your character reaction is that he can attack an enemy when he gets revealed to him.

This means in your first attack turn you can attack twice.

Step 3

This is how combat works:

The number of attack die you get in this attack is the number next to the pistol icon on your hero card.

For each attack dice you roll, your opponent gets a defense dice.

The icon next to the attack dice number indicates which dice you have to use.

For an attack dice it can be the blue or the red one. However, the blue dice is always the defense dice.

Step 4

Lets start your attack.

You roll 3 blue dice.

This is your result:

2x the shield and 1x an explosion symbol

The shield symbol means nothing on attack rolls but the explosion icon means you've landed a hit.

Step 5

Now your enemy gets to roll his defense dice for every attack rolls you did.

He rolls 3 blue (defense) dice.

This is your result:

1x an explosion symbol with an ammo icon 1x the shield and 1x a skull

The explosion symbol with an ammo icon doesn't mean anything on defense rolls. The shield symbol means that the enemy defelected your hit and he doesn't get any damage.

The skull means indicates a fail.

Step 6

Your second attack.

This is your result:

1x an explosion symbol 1x the shield and 1x an explosion symbol with an lightning .

You hit your enemy 2 times. The lightning symbol means whatever your weapon says.

In this case it means that the enemy has -1 defense dice.

Step 7

The enemy's def dice:

In our case the enemy only has 2 def dice.

He rolls 1x hit with call sign (= nothing) and 1x hit with lightning (= his action REGENERATION was triggerd)

He loses 1 armor and 1 HP and regenerates 1 HP so he has still has 4 HP but his armor was destroyed (can't be regenerated).

Step 8

Now it's the enemy's turn:

At first you need to look how many hex-fields he is away from you.

On the enemy's card you can see what he does on what distance to you.

In our case he is 1 area away. As a result he attacks with his BLASTER and moves 1 area away.

Step 9

Enemy's hit die:

1x GD-Symbol (= fail), 2x explosion symbol (= 2x hit)

Def die:

2x hit (= fail), 1x shield

Result:

agent loses 1 HP

Step 10

Your hit die:

1x Shield (= fail), 2x explosion symbol with lightning (= 2x hit & -2 def die)

Def die:

1x hit with callsign

Result:

Enemy loses 2 HP (2HP left)

Step 11

Now the enemy is 2 areas away. This means he can attack twice with his PISTOL.

Enemy's hit die:

2x hit, 1x skull (= fail)

Def die:

3x shield

Result:

Nothing happens

Step 12

2nd attack

Enemy's hit die:

1x GD-Symbol (= fail), 2x explosion symbol with lightning (= 2x hit)

Def die:

1x shield, 2x skull

Result:

-1 HP (9HP left)

Step 13

Your hit die:

1x hit with lightning, 1x hit with ammo (hat & 1 ammo less -> now 3), 1x shield

Def die:

1x hit with lightning, 1x hit with ammo symbol

Result:

Enemy loses 2 HP (0HP left)

Step 14

You successfully killed the enemy and completed this demo!
